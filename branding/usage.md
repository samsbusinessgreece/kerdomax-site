# branding/usage.md

Brand lockup rules
- Primary lockup: K monogram (logo-axis.svg) on the left, KERDOMAX wordmark (logo-text.svg) on the right.
- Spacing: maintain clear space equal to the height of the wordmark around the lockup.
- Color rules: lockup uses blue for the “KERDO” portion and beige for the “MAX” portion; do not invert in standard usage.

Iconography and variants
- App icons simple glyphs, navy navy navy (#1A2E4C)
- Monochrome navy: #1A2E4C
- Monochrome reverse: white on dark background
