# branding/colors.md

Brand palette and usage
- Slate Blue (primary, authority) Hex: #1A2E4C
- Deep Navy (backgrounds) Hex: #0F1A2E
- Warm Beige (accent) Hex: #EDE7DD
- Teal Accent Hex: #2ED3C6
- Light Text Hex: #E6E8EB

Usage tips
- Slate Blue for primary logo elements
- Warm Beige for cross-beams or accents
- Teal for CTAs and key interactions
- Use dark navy backgrounds for premium SaaS look
